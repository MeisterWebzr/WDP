    SSR Motorsports

    http://www.ssrmotorspors.com

    Site Intent

    The re-designing of SSR Motorsports informational website is to update the user interface with the appropriate principles of design to create an updated modern look and feel of the website. Overall interactiveness needs to be focused on with visual affordances implemented to manage visual flow of information on website. All text and imagery will undergo Unity/ Harmony & Color Theory tests to insure that proximity, continuation & Repetition are used to keep user interested in SSR content.

    Client Goals

    SSR Motorsports wants to re-design the website to separate its news & media publications from its wholesale and retail clients and update website with a modern interactive user interface.

    User Goals

    User will need to stay connected and updated on events, promotions and vehicle specs or modification publications for SSR Motorsports industry news. Wholesale clients will need to use imagery and vehicle specs for publishing onto their receptive websites for retail customers as well as log into the wholesale portal to purchase vehicles and equipment for events and or store presentations.

    W3C Validation Errors

    Upon Checking SSR Motorsports website with css-validator I received 21 errors. Value errors within text decorations, padding and borders. The current CSS level is 2.1. Warnings also on lines 14 & 22 "You should add a 'type' attribute with a value of 'text/css' to the 'style' element. Our re-design will validate with CSS 3.0 to maintain responsiveness across different browsers & platforms.

    Layout & Structure

    The layout of SSR Motorsports consists of top main banner with number 216 Dirt Bike popping a wheelie towards SSR Motorsports logo. Below that we have a messy navigation with 17 menu links with a grey background and a white border. Menu links are centered align also. scrolling down we have 13 different pit bikes/ scooters. 3 on the top with a black dotted border on left, bottom and right of image. moving down we have a mini facebook social link image to the left on top of the left sided navigation that runs vertical. Navigation ha 13 links with an icon just to the left of each Menu item and a grey bottom border on all menu links. Floating right we have SSR Recommendations dirt bikes consisting of 3 rows across and 3 rows down of images of the remaining dirt bikes all linking to their respective product pages

    UI/ UX Review

    Labels are not correctly styled for visual consistency as well as navigation is not streamlined for visual flow. Main CTA dirt bike has no visual affordance for interaction. No cues that separate elements to distinguish one group from another. Padding, margin and borders have in sufficient space for proximity and closure design aspects. The golden ration is not used to promote interaction among the main call to actions. left to right visual affordances are not used within navigation. Poorly rendered buttons/ links throughout website. Clarity of the textual message is confusing. Un appropriate use of fonts and textual elements within website. Product page is lacking visual flow for call to action and hierarchy for underlying elements

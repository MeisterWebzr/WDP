SSR MOTORSPORTS FACTORY LIMITED WARRANTY GUIDE

SSR Motorsports and your selling dealer thank you for selecting one of our scooters or power sports products. Our commitment to you begins with a quality product and service. This document explains in detail the warranty coverage's that apply to your new SSR Motorsports vehicle. Please read it carefully so you understand the extent of coverage on your new scooter of power sports product. For questions concerning our warranty please contact us at warraty@ssrmotorsports.com.

THE LIMITED WARRANTY FOR YOUR NEW SSR MOTORSPORTS VEHICLE

Warranty starts from date of purchase by the consumer from their SSR MOTORSPORTS dealer for all SSR MOTORSPORTS products. During the warranty policy period, SSR MOTORSPORTS will only cover all warranty parts. The dealer is required to order parts for all warranty work to be performed. ALL WARRANTY CLAIMS MUST BE SUBMITTED THROUGH YOUR PURCHASING DEALER. SSR MOTORSPORTS WILL NOT HONOR ANY WARRANTY WITHOUT NOTIFICATION FROM PURCHASING DEALER OF CLAIM.

VEHICLE OWNER'S RESPONSIBILITY

It is the responsibly of the owner to maintain and care for the vehicle as outlined in the owners manual. Owners must service and maintain the vehicle in accordance with the owners manual. All service and maintenance should be performed by an authorized SSR dealer or any reputable cycle repair shop. Period maintenance and inspections are considered REGULAR maintenance and are not subject to warranty or reimbursement.

WHEN YOUR WARRANTY BEGINS

Your Limited Warranty Start Date is the day you take delivery of your new SSR scooter or powersports vehicle. PURCHASING A UNIT IN THE CRATE OR WITHOUT THE PROPER SET UP WILL VOID ALL WARRANTY COVERAGE!

LIMITED WARRANTY

  • All new SSR MOTORCYCLES are covered with 1 YEAR LIMITED WARRANTY.
  • All new SSR SCOOTERS are covered with a 6 MONTH LIMITED WARRANTY.
  • All new SSR PIT-BIKES, ATV's and UTV's are covered with a 3 MONTH LIMITED WARRANTY.

Only SSR MOTORSPORTS authorized dealers can submit warranty claim not consumers. The warranty registration needs to be on file with SSR MOTORSPORTS before a warranty claim can be processed, unless the claim is due to shipping or crate damage and the unit has not yet been sold.

SSR MOTORSPORTS DOES NOT COVER THE LABOR OR SHIPPING REQUIRED TO REMOVE OR SHIP THE VEHICLE OR PART FOR WARRANTY REPLACEMENT.
